About the event

Rubaroo is a two-day National Level Cultural Fest witnessing participation from over 5000+ students every year from all across the nation. It is acknowledged as one of the biggest university cultural fest in the region. We have had prominent and accomplished artists like Bohemia, Tochi Raina, Javed Ali, Shalmali Kholgade, Shilpa Rao, Eka Band, Euphoria Band and Darshan Raval have graced the fest over the years and wound want your participation too.
Being held on the 28th and 29th of April, RUBAROO’23 is all set to blow your minds. From fashion to drama, dance to music, fun events to literary events, every part of this festival ensures to create a mesmerizing impression on your minds.

About SRMIST


SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) NCR Campus is one of the leading, world-class institutes in the nation, offering a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Science & Humanities. With over 20,000 students and 1,500 faculty members, SRMIST was ranked amongst the Top 30 institutes in the nation in the latest edition of the NIRF university rankings and is currently accredited with an A++ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) . For over 25 years, SRMIST has been a part of the 40 year old SRM Group of Educational Institutes that are known for providing quality education in frontier areas.
