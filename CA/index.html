What is CA Program

As part of the student campus ambassador program, RUBAROO offers students the chance to represent and promote the organization at their colleges and universities. Their responsibilities comprise developing our presence and promoting THOMSO events among students and educators by serving as a link between their colleges and us.


Why to become CA

1. The most significant opportunity to represent your college in the fest at north India, improving your leadership qualities.

2. As Rubaroo invites celebrities from a diverse field, you will get a chance to interact with the stars.
