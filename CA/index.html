WHAT IS CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM?

As part of the student campus ambassador program, RUBAROO offers students the chance to represent and promote the organization at their colleges and universities. Their responsibilities comprise developing our presence and promoting RUBAROO events among students and educators by serving as a link between their colleges and us.



WHY TO BECOME CAMPUS AMBASSADOR ?

The most significant opportunity to represent your college in one of the biggest fest at north India, improving your leadership qualities. As Rubaroo invites many celebrities from a diverse field, you will get a chance to interact with the stars.
